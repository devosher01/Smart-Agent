Thesis 01

The 50% Shift

50%

By the end of 2026, we predict that more than 50% of global web traffic will be driven by autonomous agents, not humans.

Current subscription models fail for non-human actors. This is why we implemented the x402 Protocolâ€”a standard for HTTP 402 Payment Required status codes that allows agents to negotiate and pay for data access instantly, on-demand, without credit cards or login forms.

Thesis 02

The T-REX Protocol

T-REX

As everything becomes automated, establishing trust is critical. We use ERC-8004 to register "Trustless Agents" on-chain, proving their execution logic and reputation.

Furthermore, for regulated actions like selling Real World Assets (RWAs) or performing KYC, we want to integrate the ERC-3643 T-REX Standard. This ensures that every automated transaction is compliant, permissioned, and tied to a validated identity.
